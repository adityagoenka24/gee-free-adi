The Problem

Why smart students still
struggle with GRE Quant

It's not a knowledge gap. It's a pattern-recognition gap. Every platform shows you the solution — almost none show you why you specifically got it wrong.

🔁
You keep making the same mistakes
You review the solution, understand it, then make the same error two days later. Because you never addressed the underlying trap — you just learned the answer.
📉
Your score plateaus despite practice
More practice without targeted feedback is just expensive repetition. Without knowing which subtopics drain your accuracy, you practice the same things you're already decent at.
⏳
Practice without real time pressure
Unlimited time practice creates false confidence. The GRE gives you 1 min 45 sec per question. If you've never practiced at that pace, exam day will feel completely different.
